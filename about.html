Who Are We?

Vertigo Clothing Inc. is a modern fashion apparel company that originated out of Pretoria, South Africa in 2017. Being a relative newcomer to the market Vertigo has faced a lot of challenges in trying to keep up with the 'big boys'. Despite this challenge Vertigo can take solice in knowing they are now considered one of them through hard work and a brand whose focus has always been on providing customers with designer apparel at a efficient cost. Headed by CEO Harry Redknapp Vertigo was just a dream that has now turned into reality, with a total of 57 stores nationwide and a further 10 in Africa Vertigo is continuing to grow and it doesn't look to be stopping anytime soon.
